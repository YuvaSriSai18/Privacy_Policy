SRMAP MESS Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy page of the SRMAP MESS app, developed by Yuva Sri Sai. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Our app may collect the following types of information :

  • Personal Information :We may collect your name, email address, and other contact details when you contact us for support or assistance.
  • Device Information :We automatically collect information about your device, including the operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data : We collect information about how you use the app, including interactions with features and content.
  • Payment Information : If you make purchases within the app, we may collect payment information, such as credit card details or other payment account information. This information is securely processed by our payment service providers and is not stored on our servers.
  • Authentication Data :When you log in or create an account within the app, we may collect authentication data such as usernames, passwords, or other credentials to securely authenticate and verify your identity.
  • Device Location : With your consent, we may collect your device's location information to provide location-based services or functionalities within the app.
  • Advertisements : We may collect information related to the advertisements displayed within the app, including interactions with ads, to improve ad relevancy and effectiveness.

How we use your Information

We see the collected information for the following purposes :

  • Providing and improving app functionality, including personalized features and content.
  • Processing payments and transactions securely.
  • Authenticating users and maintaining account security.
  • Offering location-based services or features, with your consent.
  • Displaying relevant advertisements and measuring ad performance.
  • Understanding user preferences and behavior to enhance user experience.
  • Sending relevant notifications, updates, and promotional materials.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with third-party service providers for the following purposes:

  • Analytics :We may use third-party analytics services to analyze user behavior and app performance.
  • Marketing :We may share your email address with marketing partners for promotional campaigns. However, we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information :

  • Access and Update :You can access and update your personal information through the app's settings.
  • Opt-Out :You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the instructions in the emails. You can also adjust app notification settings.
  • User Rights :You have the right to request access, rectification, erasure, or restriction of your personal data. You can exercise these rights by contacting us.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access and use. However, no data transmission over the internet or mobile networks can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to us.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to remove the information.
